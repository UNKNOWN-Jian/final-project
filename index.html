About Me

Hello, my name is James Ian Escabas, and I am a 20-year-old resident of Poblacion Cordova, Cebu. Growing up in this vibrant community has instilled in me a deep appreciation for culture, resilience, and the power of stories, which fuels my aspiration to become a writer someday. With a passion for the written word and a dedication to honing my craft, I am committed to expressing ideas, emotions, and experiences that resonate with others and inspire positive change. My journey toward this goal is driven by a strong desire to craft meaningful narratives that can touch lives and evoke thought, making my mark in the literary world. Currently, I am pursuing my studies at Cordova National High School, where I am literacy-inclined and actively engaged in academic pursuits, and I am now embarking on my second year as a college student taking up Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT). This educational path not only broadens my technical skills but also complements my creative ambitions, allowing me to explore diverse ways of storytelling through digital media and technology. As I continue to grow academically and personally, I remain dedicated to my dream of becoming a writer, believing that perseverance and passion are key to transforming aspirations into reality.
